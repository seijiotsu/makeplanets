A note from the developer:

Hi, thank you for trying out my new tool, Make Planets. Make Planets is my attempt at giving back to the worldbuilding community by helping scifi authors and other folk design their own plausible planetary systems. Even though I stress that this tool is in very early development, I hope that at this point it is usable enough to be of interest to early adopters. I am confident that, with time, this tool will grow to be even more feature-complete and bug-free than it currently is. Once again -- thank you, and enjoy Make Planets v0.11
